Purchasing User Seats and Storage Space

How to Purchase User Seats or Storage Space

    • Settings Dialog At the top of Central, click your user name, then click Settings. The Settings dialog opens. Click the Server tab. Click Buy Seats or Buy Space.

    • Storage and Usage Widget If you’ve added the Storage and Usage widget (see About Widgets), click to open the Home page. In the widget, click Buy Seats or Buy Space.

    The Purchase dialog opens.

    Note

  2. In the Seats field, select the number of user seats you want to add to your license.

    Note

  3. In the Space field, select the amount of storage space (in GB) that you want to add to your license.

    Note

    Central calculates the total cost and displays it below.

  5. In the Billing Information dialog, click Continue.
  6. In the Purchase dialog, click Continue. The Confirm Purchase dialog opens. A message shows you the number of user seats and/or the amount of storage space you are purchasing, as well as the total amount of the purchase.
  7. Click Complete Purchase. Your card will be charged.
 Notes

If you need to renew your annual subscription, please contact MadCap Software Sales.

If you need to cancel your subscription, you can simply delete your credit card information from the system, as described above.
